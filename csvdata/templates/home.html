Welcome to CSV plotter !

CSV plotter is a simple Visualization tool to help you comprehend your CSV (comma-separated values) data graphically !
This tool allows you to render and plot your CSV data into various graphs i.e. Histogram, Bar Graph, Scatter graph,Pie Chart...

You are 5 steps away to see Beautiful grahps from your CSV data !

  1. Upload you CSV file and give appropriate title to it
  2. After Submitting, you can see details of your CSV file like total records, columns
  3. Next Click at bottom to see Graphs List or choose another CSV file
  4. Select Approriate Graph you want to plot from given choices
  5. Almost Done !, Enter your desired records range and columns to see you beautiful graph
